What this produces
  • One tidy CSV: run, run_index, timestep, metric, n, mean, median
  • metric ∈ {gene, learning, social, meme, population}
  • One row per (run × snapshot × metric). run_index distinguishes the ~48 individual runs within a condition — this is the unit of replication for statistics.
  • Trait rows give the per-run mean/median collapsed from the 20-bucket histogram; population rows store the value in mean.
  • "Export ALL" chains through every condition and downloads a single biodegen_perrun_all.csv. Feed that to data/stats_analysis.py.
